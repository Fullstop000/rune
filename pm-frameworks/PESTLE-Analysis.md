# PESTLE Analysis (PESTLE 分析)

**Category:** Definition (问题定义)

**Framework ID:** #16

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-pestle

---

## Overview

PESTLE analyzes six macro dimensions—Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental—to scan the external environment and identify opportunities and threats.

**Origin:** Francis Aguilar's 1967 ETPS model

---

## Six Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **P – Political** | Government policy, regulation, political stability, tax policy, international relations |
| **E – Economic** | GDP growth, inflation, purchasing power, interest rates, employment, exchange rates |
| **S – Social** | Demographics, cultural trends, lifestyle changes, social values, education levels |
| **T – Technological** | New technologies, adoption speed, R&D trends, automation |
| **L – Legal** | Consumer protection, intellectual property, data privacy regulations |
| **E – Environmental** | Climate change, sustainability pressures, carbon regulations, ESG trends |

---

## Six-Step Process

1. **Scope Definition (30 min)** — Define decision, geographic scope, time window
2. **Information Gathering (2-3 days)** — Research each dimension with 2-4 hours per dimension
3. **Impact Assessment (half-day workshop)** — Rate impact level and probability
4. **Opportunity/Threat Conversion (2-4 hours)** — Translate factors into product implications
5. **Monitoring Mechanisms (ongoing)** — Assign responsibility, define sources, set trigger thresholds

---

## Case Study: Netflix

**China Market Failure (2016-2019):**
- Political: Content regulation conflicted with global strategy
- Legal: Internet broadcasting licenses proved unobtainable
- Technological: VPN restrictions blocked hybrid entry approaches
- Social: Local platforms had deeply embedded user habits

**Southeast Asia Success:**
- Economic: Mobile-first pricing (India: 149 rupees/month)
- Social: Local language content investment
- Technological: Compression optimization for 3G networks
- Legal: Proactive regulatory relationship building

## Skill Download

**File:** `skills/pestle.md`

**Skill Content:**

```markdown
# PESTLE Analysis

Systematically scan six macro-environmental dimensions for external forces that create opportunities or threats.

## Steps

1. **Political** — government policy, trade regulations, political stability, tax policy.
2. **Economic** — growth rates, inflation, exchange rates, disposable income trends.
3. **Social** — demographics, cultural trends, lifestyle changes, education levels.
4. **Technological** — emerging tech, R&D activity, automation, digital infrastructure.
5. **Legal** — employment law, consumer protection, data privacy, IP regulations.
6. **Environmental** — climate, sustainability mandates, resource scarcity, waste regulations.

## Output Format

PESTLE table with key factors per dimension, impact assessment (positive/negative, high/medium/low), time horizon, and recommended strategic responses.
```
