# Go-To-Market (GTM 上市策略)

**Category:** Growth (增长策略)

**Framework ID:** #66

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-gtm

---

## Overview

完整作战计划，用于向客户推出产品并进入市场。回答五个核心问题：卖给谁、为什么选你、怎么定价、如何触达、用什么方式获客。

**Core Value:** 好产品也会死在发布日——产品质量只是成功的前提，而非充分条件。

---

## Five Elements

| # | Element | Core Question |
|---|---------|---------------|
| 01 | Target Market | 谁是你的 ICP？越具体越好 |
| 02 | Value Proposition | 为什么选你而非竞品？聚焦客户结果 |
| 03 | Pricing & Packaging | 如何定价？Freemium/席位/用量/成果 |
| 04 | Distribution Channels | 客户在哪？直销/内销/渠道/应用市场 |
| 05 | Customer Acquisition | PLG、SLG 还是混合模式？ |

---

## PLG vs SLG

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **PLG (Slack path)** | 免费层 → 病毒式团队传播 → Land and Expand → 企业化升级 |
| **SLG (Salesforce path)** | 企业销售 → 专属 AE → 自上而下实施 → 续约与扩展 |

---

## Case Study: Slack (2013-2015)

- Core bet: 先攻开发者和技术人员，让他们把 Slack 带进每家公司
- 12个月内 ARR: $7M
- 前70%收入 CAC: ≈$0
- 早期销售团队规模: 0人

```markdown
description: Build a go-to-market launch plan covering target audience, positioning, channels, and pricing
---

# Go-To-Market Strategy

Plan how to bring a product to market by defining who to sell to, how to position, where to reach them, and at what price.

## Steps

1. Define **target segment** -- who is the ideal early adopter and what is their urgent problem?
2. Craft **positioning** -- category, differentiation, and key message (use: "For [target] who [need], [product] is [category] that [benefit]")
3. Select **channels** -- rank acquisition channels by reach, cost, and fit
4. Set **pricing** -- model (freemium, subscription, usage), price point, and packaging
5. Plan the **launch** -- timeline, milestones, success metrics, and first 90 days

## Output Format

- **Target:** [Segment + persona]
- **Positioning:** For [target] who [need], [product] is [category] that [benefit]
- **Channels:** [Ranked list with rationale]
- **Pricing:** [Model + price point]
- **Launch milestones:** [Week-by-week plan]
- **Success metrics:** [KPIs for first 90 days]
```
