# How Now Wow Matrix (How Now Wow 矩阵)

**Category:** Ideation (创意生成)

**Framework ID:** #27

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-how-now-wow

---

## Overview

A creative prioritization matrix that uses two dimensions—originality (novelty) and feasibility—to categorize post-brainstorming ideas into four quadrants, enabling teams to make objective prioritization decisions in 30 minutes.

---

## The Four Quadrants

| Quadrant | Characteristics | Action |
|----------|----------------|--------|
| **Wow** | High creativity + High feasibility | Prioritize and execute |
| **Now** | Low creativity + High feasibility | Quick wins, fast experiments |
| **How** | High creativity + Low feasibility | Archive for future quarters |
| **Dead zone** | Low creativity + Low feasibility | Discard |

---

## Key Steps

1. **Complete brainstorming** – Each idea on separate sticky note
2. **Individual scoring (10 min)** – Silent scoring: novelty (1-5) + feasibility (1-5)
3. **Collective plotting (15 min)** – Plot on matrix, discuss discrepancies
4. **Assign actions (10 min)** – Select top 3 WOWs, designate DRIs and next steps

---

## Case Study: IDEO × Kaiser Permanente (2003)

Patient experience redesign. From 100+ ideas, the WOW was "patient receives printed visit summary before leaving." This later became U.S. healthcare regulation.

## Skill Download

**File:** `skills/how-now-wow.md`

**Skill Content:**

```markdown
# How Now Wow Matrix

Categorize ideas into three buckets based on novelty and feasibility. Use when you have many ideas and need to decide which to pursue first.

## Steps

1. List all candidate ideas on sticky notes or a shared board.
2. Draw a 2x2 matrix with axes: Novelty (low→high) and Feasibility (low→high).
3. Classify each idea: **Now** (low novelty, high feasibility — easy wins), **Wow** (high novelty, high feasibility — breakthrough opportunities), **How** (high novelty, low feasibility — future bets needing research).
4. Discard or park ideas that are both low novelty and low feasibility.
5. Prioritize Wow ideas first, Now ideas for quick wins, and How ideas for R&D backlog.

## Output Format

A 2x2 matrix visual with each idea placed in its category, plus a prioritized action list with owners and timelines for the top Wow and Now ideas.
```
