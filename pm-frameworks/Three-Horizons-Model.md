# Three Horizons Model (Three Horizons 三视野)

**Category:** Systems Thinking (系统思维)

**Framework ID:** #74

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-three-horizons

---

## Overview

H1: optimize core business; H2: build new growth engines; H3: explore future options—maintaining innovation portfolio balance.

**Creator:** McKinsey & Company (Mehrdad Baghai, Stephen Coley, David White)

**Year:** 1999 (book "The Alchemy of Growth")

---

## Three Horizons

| Horizon | Focus | Timeframe | Certainty |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|
| **H1** | Core business defense/optimization | 1-2 years | High |
| **H2** | Emerging growth business scaling | 2-5 years | Medium |
| **H3** | Future seed business exploration | 5+ years | High potential |

---

## Resource Allocation

**70 : 20 : 10** (H1 : H2 : H3)

---

## Case Study: Amazon (2006-2016)

| Horizon | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| **H1** | Core e-commerce, Prime membership, "Day 1" culture |
| **H2** | AWS (internal → commercial), 2023 revenue $90B+ |
| **H3** | Kindle (H3→H2 success), Alexa/Echo |

**Result:** $90B+ AWS, 200M+ Prime members

# Three Horizons Framework

Manage innovation across three time horizons to balance short-term performance with long-term growth.

## Steps

1. **H1 -- Core business:** List initiatives that defend and extend today's revenue. Optimize, scale, protect margins
2. **H2 -- Emerging opportunities:** List bets on adjacent markets or business models gaining early traction
3. **H3 -- Exploration:** List moonshots, R&D, and experiments with high uncertainty but transformative potential
4. Assess **resource allocation** across horizons (common target: 70/20/10)
5. Ensure H2 has a clear **graduation path** to H1 and H3 feeds H2 pipeline
6. Review quarterly: promote, kill, or pivot initiatives across horizons

## Output Format

| Horizon | Initiatives | Investment % | Time Frame | Success Metric |
|---------|------------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| H1 - Core | [List] | [X]% | 0-12 months | [Revenue/margin] |
| H2 - Emerging | [List] | [X]% | 12-24 months | [Traction KPI] |
| H3 - Exploration | [List] | [X]% | 24-36 months | [Learning/validation] |

**Balance assessment:** [Current vs. target allocation]
