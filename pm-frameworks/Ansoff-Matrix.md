# Ansoff Matrix (Ansoff 增长矩阵)

**Category:** Growth (增长策略)

**Framework ID:** #71

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-ansoff-matrix

---

## Overview

Four quadrants: Existing/New Products × Existing/New Markets—defining four growth strategies: penetration, development, product, diversification.

**Creator:** Igor Ansoff

**Year:** 1957

---

## The Four Quadrants

| Quadrant | Products | Markets | Strategy |
|---------|----------|---------|----------|
| **Market Penetration** | Existing | Existing | Sell more to existing markets |
| **Market Development** | Existing | New | Sell existing to new markets |
| **Product Development** | New | Existing | Sell new products to existing markets |
| **Diversification** | New | New | Sell new products to new markets |

---

## Risk Level

Risk increases from Market Penetration to Diversification.

## Skill Content

```markdown
# Ansoff Growth Matrix

Evaluate growth strategies using the Ansoff Matrix of market and product dimensions.

## Steps

1. **Market Penetration** (existing product, existing market) — increase share through pricing, promotion, distribution, or loyalty.
2. **Product Development** (new product, existing market) — extend the product line, add features, or create adjacent offerings for current customers.
3. **Market Development** (existing product, new market) — enter new geographies, segments, or channels with your current product.
4. **Diversification** (new product, new market) — launch new offerings in unfamiliar markets; highest risk, highest potential reward.
5. Assess each quadrant's risk level, required capabilities, and expected ROI to select the optimal growth path.

## Output Format

2x2 Ansoff matrix with specific initiatives per quadrant, risk assessment, capability gap analysis, and recommended growth strategy with rationale.
```
