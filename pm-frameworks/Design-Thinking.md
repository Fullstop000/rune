# Design Thinking (设计思维)

**Category:** Discovery (用户洞察)

**Framework ID:** #01

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-design-thinking

---

## Overview

"五步循环，从理解人到解决问题。不是一条直线，而是随时可以折回的探索路径。"

**Origin:** IDEO · Tim Brown (2008 HBR article)

**Phases:** Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test

**Duration:** Workshop 1–2 days or rapid 4-hour version

---

## What It Solves

- New product exploration
- Service redesign
- Organizational innovation (把员工当作用户发现根因)
- Insufficient user research
- Direction confusion (从第一原则重新审视核心价值)
- Cross-functional alignment (建立共同语言)

---

## Five-Step Framework

| Step | Name | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Empathize (共情)** | 深度访谈、实地观察、体验用户处境 |
| 2 | **Define (定义)** | 整合洞察、撰写 POV 陈述、确定设计挑战 |
| 3 | **Ideate (创意)** | 发散头脑风暴、How Might We 提问、评估筛选 |
| 4 | **Prototype (原型)** | 快速低保真制作、聚焦核心假设 |
| 5 | **Test (测试)** | 与真实用户测试、观察行为而非收集意见 |

**核心原则：** 原型和测试的目的不是验证，而是学习。测试发现用户根本在意不同的事时，应返回共情阶段。

## Case Study: IDEO × Bank of America "Keep the Change"

美国银行委托 IDEO 为非储蓄习惯人群设计储蓄产品。项目完整执行设计思维五步，最终诞生银行史上增长最快的储蓄账户之一。

**核心洞察：** 人们会把找零扔进罐子里，说明「存钱」的阻力来自「主动性」而非「意愿」。

**最终方案：** 每笔消费四舍五入到整元，差额自动存入储蓄账户。

**Results:**
- 250 万新开账户
- $7 亿新增储蓄
- 99% 用户选择保留

## Usage Guidelines

**✓ Do:**
- 快速迭代原型，每轮目的只验证一个核心假设
- 从极端用户身上学习
- 允许失败，把每次测试当作学习机会
- 组建多学科团队共同参与

**✗ Don't:**
- 跳过共情阶段直接出方案
- 只做一轮原型就认为验证完毕
- 把设计思维当成流程表单填写
- 测试阶段问用户「你喜不喜欢」而非观察真实行为

## Skill Download

**File:** `skills/design-thinking.md`

**Skill Content:**

```markdown
# Design Thinking (IDEO)

A human-centered innovation framework that cycles through empathy, problem definition, ideation, prototyping, and testing to arrive at solutions users actually need.

## Steps

1. **Empathize** — Observe and interview users to understand their experiences, behaviors, and motivations deeply.
2. **Define** — Synthesize findings into a clear problem statement that captures the core user need.
3. **Ideate** — Generate a wide range of possible solutions without judgment; quantity over quality.
4. **Prototype** — Build low-fidelity representations of the most promising ideas to make them tangible.
5. **Test** — Put prototypes in front of real users, gather feedback, and iterate back to any earlier step.

## Output Format

A structured report containing: user insights summary, problem statement, top 3-5 solution concepts with sketches or descriptions, prototype plan, and test findings with next-iteration recommendations.
```
