# Innovation Portfolio Balance (创新组合平衡)

**Category:** Systems Thinking (系统思维)

**Framework ID:** #81

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-innovation-portfolio-balance.html

---

## Overview

Dynamically allocate resources between "exploiting existing" and "exploring new"—maintaining portfolio balance.

---

## The Balance

| Focus | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Exploiting** | Optimize and extend current business |
| **Exploring** | Discover and build new opportunities |

---

## Framework

| Horizon | Allocation | Focus |
|---------|------------|-------|
| **H1** | 70% | Core business (exploitation) |
| **H2** | 20% | Adjacent opportunities |
| **H3** | 10% | Transformational bets |

---

## Key Challenge

Not over-investing in current success (exploitation) vs. over-investing in uncertain future (exploration)

## Skill Content

```markdown
# Innovation Portfolio Balance (创新组合平衡)

Balance exploitation of current business with exploration of new opportunities.

## Steps

1. **Audit current portfolio** — Map existing initiatives by horizon (H1 core, H2 adjacent, H3 transformational)
2. **Assess balance ratio** — Calculate % allocation across horizons (typical: 70/20/10)
3. **Identify imbalance signals** — Look for over-investment in exploitation (complacency) or exploration (scatter)
4. **Rebalance resources** — Shift budget and talent toward underrepresented horizons
5. **Establish review cadence** — Quarterly portfolio reviews to maintain动态平衡

## Output Format

A portfolio matrix showing: Initiative Name, Horizon (H1/H2/H3), Resource Allocation, Key Metrics, and Balance Status (green/yellow/red).
```
