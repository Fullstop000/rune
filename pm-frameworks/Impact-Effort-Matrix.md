# Impact Effort Matrix (Impact Effort 矩阵)

**Category:** Execution (执行落地)

**Framework ID:** #49

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-impact-effort-matrix

---

## Overview

A 2×2 prioritization tool using two dimensions—"Impact" and "Effort"—to identify high-value, low-cost quick wins while avoiding low-value, high-cost resource traps.

**Type:** Priority visualization tool

**Duration:** Team meeting 30-60 minutes

---

## The Four Quadrants

| Quadrant | Impact | Effort | Action |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|
| ⭐ **Quick Wins** | High | Low | Do immediately |
| 🎯 **Major Projects** | High | High | Plan and schedule |
| 🗒 **Fill-ins** | Low | Low | Do when有空 |
| ❌ **Time Sinks** | Low | High | Avoid/拒绝 |

---

## Meeting Process

1. List all items on Post-it notes
2. Team members score independently (avoid anchoring)
3. Place on matrix and discuss disagreements
4. Make decisions by quadrant

---

## Case Study: Basecamp/37signals

- **Duration:** 6-week development cycle
- **Quick Wins:** 4 items (file upload optimization, keyboard shortcuts)
- **Major Projects:** 3 items (search feature, permissions redesign)
- **Time Sinks:** 6 items publicly rejected
- **Fill-ins:** 10 items in gaps

---

## Key Recommendations

1. Define "Impact" clearly before starting
2. Evaluate independently before group discussion
3. Don't over-precise—it's a conversation tool, not a spreadsheet
4. Can't replace strategic judgment
5. Re-evaluate quarterly
