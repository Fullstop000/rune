## Overview

Nassim Taleb's concept: truly strong systems don't just resist volatility—they benefit and improve from uncertainty and stress.

**Creator:** Nassim Nicholas Taleb

**Year:** 2012 ("Antifragile" book)

# Antifragile Design

Design systems that gain from volatility, uncertainty, and stress -- not just survive them.

## Steps

1. Identify **fragile points** -- where does the current system break under stress?
2. Design for **optionality** -- can failure happen without catastrophic consequences?
3. Add **feedback loops** -- systems that learn and adapt from failures
4. Build in **redundancy** -- backup systems that activate under stress
5. Remove **single points of failure** -- diversify dependencies
6. Stress test with **chaos experiments** -- introduce small failures to strengthen resilience
