# Value Proposition Canvas (价值主张画布)

**Category:** Ideation (创意生成)

**Framework ID:** #28

**Source:** https://pmframe.works/framework-value-proposition-canvas

---

## Overview

一张工具，两个侧面。左边是你能提供的，右边是用户真正需要的。当两侧精准咬合，产品才真正有价值。

**Creator:** Alexander Osterwalder & Yves Pigneur

**Year:** 2014 年《Value Proposition Design》

---

## Two Sides

### Customer Profile (Right Side)

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Customer Jobs** | 功能性、社交性、情感性任务 |
| **Pains** | 阻碍任务完成的烦恼与风险 |
| **Gains** | 用户期望获得的结果与好处 |

### Value Map (Left Side)

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Products & Services** | 产品功能 |
| **Pain Relievers** | 如何解决用户痛点 |
| **Gain Creators** | 如何创造用户收益 |

---

## Fit Concept

衡量两侧匹配程度的概念：当价值地图能够解决客户档案中最重要的工作、痛点和收益时，即达到 fit。

---

## Steps

1. **选定客户细分** — 每张画布只针对一类客户
2. **列出用户任务** — 从功能、社交、情感三类着手
3. **识别痛点** — 任务完成过程中的挫折与风险
4. **描述收益期望** — 用户期望的结果与惊喜体验
5. **填写价值地图** — 列出产品功能，对应填写痛点解药与收益制造器
6. **寻找契合点** — 标注真正解决最重要痛点/任务的功能

---

## Case Study: Spotify (2008年产品定义阶段)

核心洞察："用户不是想要'拥有'音乐，而是想要随时能听到。"

通过画布分析识别出：
- **任务：** 随时听任意歌曲、社交分享、发现新音乐
- **痛点：** 购买成本高、下载等待、盗版风险
- **收益：** 零等待播放、个性化推荐、合法无负担

```markdown
description: Use when aligning product offerings with specific customer needs and desires

# Value Proposition Canvas

Map the fit between what your product offers (Value Map) and what your customer actually needs (Customer Profile) to ensure product-market alignment.

## Steps

1. **Customer Jobs** — List the functional, social, and emotional jobs the customer is trying to get done.
2. **Customer Pains** — Identify the negative outcomes, risks, and obstacles the customer experiences before, during, or after getting the job done.
3. **Customer Gains** — Describe the outcomes and benefits the customer desires, expects, or would be surprised by.
4. **Pain Relievers** — For each pain, describe how your product eliminates or reduces it.
5. **Gain Creators** — For each gain, describe how your product produces or enables it.
6. **Products and Services** — List the specific offerings that deliver the pain relievers and gain creators.

## Output Format

Two side-by-side sections: Customer Profile (Jobs, Pains, Gains) and Value Map (Products/Services, Pain Relievers, Gain Creators). Draw explicit lines showing which offerings address which customer needs. Highlight strong fits and gaps.
```
