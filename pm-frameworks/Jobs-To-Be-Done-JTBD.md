# Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)

**Category:** Discovery (用户洞察)

## Description

Users "hire" products to accomplish life tasks. Focusing on the task itself rather than product features reveals genuine needs.

## Core Concept

Understanding that users don't buy products—they "hire" them to get jobs done in their lives. The focus is on the functional, emotional, and social "jobs" users are trying to accomplish.

## Key Principles

- Focus on the job, not the product
- Understand the full context of use
- Identify the progress users want to make
- Look for functional, emotional, and social dimensions

## When to Use

- Product strategy and roadmap planning
- Identifying unmet user needs
- Innovation and new product development
- Market segmentation
